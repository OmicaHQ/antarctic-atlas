Antarctic Research Atlas

An interactive research platform for exploring the Antarctic Ice Sheet

This project transforms a detailed review paper:

> Noble, T. L. et al. (2020). *The Sensitivity of the Antarctic Ice Sheet to a Changing Climate: Past, Present, and Future.* Reviews of Geophysics, 58, e2019RG000663.

into an interactive Streamlit application that allows users to explore Antarctic research visually and interactively.

---
🔹 Features

- 🌌 **Research Universe Explorer** – Explore the key concepts and relationships in Antarctic Ice Sheet research.
- 🛰️ **Antarctic System Explorer** – Visualize satellite observations and compare different glaciers and ice shelves.
- 🎨 **AI Visualizer** – Generate scientific stories and animations based on the paper (supports API backends online).
- 🧪 **Mini Research Lab** – Conduct small experiments and data analysis interactively.
- 📄 **Read Raw Paper** – Access the full review paper PDF and navigate it within the app.
- 🧭 **Research Directions** – Explore suggested research questions and directions in Antarctic science.

---

⚡ Note

- The local AI model (Ollama) only works on the developer's machine.  
- Online users can use the **DeepSeek API** or **OpenAI API** for AI-driven features.
